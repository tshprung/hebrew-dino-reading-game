Privacy Policy for Dino Reading Hebrew

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Dino Reading Hebrew ("the App") is an educational Hebrew reading game designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our young users and their families.

1. Children's Privacy & COPPA Compliance

The App is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, require, or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from children, such as names, emails, phone numbers, or precise locations.

2. Information We Collect Automatically (Anonymous Analytics)

To improve the game experience, fix bugs, and understand gameplay progression, the App uses Google Analytics for Firebase. This service collects strictly anonymous, aggregated, and non-personal data, including:

  • App performance diagnostics (e.g., crash logs).
  • General device information (e.g., device model, OS version).
  • Anonymous gameplay events (e.g., when a level starts, when a mistake is made, or when a station is completed, along with completion time).

Important: All advertising identifiers (Ad-IDs), ad tracking, and ad personalization signals are explicitly disabled in the App's configuration to ensure maximum privacy for children.

3. Local Storage

The child's gameplay progress and unlocked levels are stored locally on the device's storage. This information remains on the device and is never uploaded to our servers.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Anonymous analytics data is processed securely by Google Firebase solely to help us monitor and improve the App's stability and educational value.

5. Security

We care about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. The anonymous analytics data collected via Google Firebase is encrypted in transit.
