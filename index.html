Privacy Policy for Dino Reading Hebrew

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Dino Reading Hebrew is an educational Hebrew reading game for children.

The app does not require login, does not show ads, and does not collect personal information.

The app stores progress locally on the device only. This information is not sent to us or to any server.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, social features, location access, contacts, camera, microphone, or account access.
